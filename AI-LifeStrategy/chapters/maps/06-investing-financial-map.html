Leadership Product-focused, engineering-driven CEO more visible on Twitter than product in production

🎢 The Hype Cycle Investment Rules

  1. The revolution is real, timing is unpredictable

    AI will transform the economy — but nobody knows which company wins when. Index funds own all the horses in the race.

  2. Picks-and-shovels is safer

    Cloud providers, chip manufacturers, data infrastructure — not the latest AI startup. Sell shovels during the gold rush.

  3. If your cab driver knows, you're late

    By the time an AI stock is mainstream news, most early gains are priced in. NVIDIA was 2019, not 2024.

  4. Max 10% in any single trend

    Even if you're right about AI, 80% of your portfolio in AI stocks is concentration risk that can destroy you.

🎲 Alternative Investments — Honest Assessment

🚀

Potential 10-100×, but 90% of startups fail. Only invest money you can lose completely. Most fail from market, not tech.
₿
Cryptocurrency
High volatility, speculative. Cap at 5% of portfolio. Price driven by sentiment, not technology quality.
🏠
Real Estate
REITs for passive ($100+). House hacking for active. Remote work is shifting demand from tech hubs to lifestyle cities.
💼
Your Own Business
Best alternative investment — you control the outcome, capture 100% upside, and your dev edge actually matters here.

📖 Key Terms

Three-Fund Portfolio
US total market + international + bonds. Simple, diversified, effective for most investors.
FIRE
Financial Independence, Retire Early — accumulate 25× annual expenses, live off 4% withdrawals.
Coast FIRE
Enough invested that compound growth handles retirement; current income only covers current expenses.
Index Fund
A fund holding all stocks in an index (e.g., S&P 500). Average market returns that beat most stock-pickers.
REIT
Real Estate Investment Trust — buy shares of real estate portfolios like stocks. Passive, low minimum.
Picks-and-Shovels Strategy
Invest in infrastructure providers (cloud, chips) rather than speculative end-user AI companies.
Runway Number
How many months you can survive if all income stops. Emergency fund ÷ monthly expenses.
Savings Rate
Savings ÷ income × 100. Target: 20%+. The single biggest lever in building wealth.

🧪 Quick Quiz

Test Your Knowledge

What percentage of active fund managers underperform the S&P 500 over 20 years?

90%. This is why index investing beats almost every "expert" stock-picker over the long term.

What's the priority order of the Financial Stack?

Emergency fund → kill high-interest debt → capture employer match → invest → optimize.

What's your Full FIRE number if you spend $60K/year?

$1.5M (annual expenses × 25). That's the amount where 4% annual withdrawals cover your living costs.

Why is checking your portfolio daily a bad idea?

Frequent checking leads to emotional decisions — panic-selling during dips. Investors who check less often earn higher returns.

What's the "picks-and-shovels" strategy for AI investing?

Invest in infrastructure (cloud, chips, data) rather than speculative AI startups. During a gold rush, sell shovels.

📌 Key Takeaways

If You Remember Nothing Else

  • Fix the foundation first: emergency fund, debt, employer match — in that order.
  • A three-fund index portfolio, automated and ignored, beats 90% of stock-pickers.
  • Your developer edge is defensive — spot fakes, don't try to pick winners.
  • FOMO is not an investment strategy. Max 10% in any single trend.
  • AI accelerates FIRE: higher income, lower costs, better analysis tools.
  • Protect what you build: insurance, will, beneficiaries — one boring Saturday.
  • Your best alternative investment is your own business — you control the outcome.
  • Automate everything. The less you touch your investments, the better they perform.
