📅 The 90-Day Plan

  1. Month 1: Foundation

    🏃 Health habits (sleep, walk, strength) · 💰 Financial audit & runway number · 💼 Career moat statement · 🧠 Start 12-week learning sprint · 🤝 Map relationships, reach out to 3 dormant connections

  2. Month 2: Build

    💵 Launch side income experiment · ✍️ Publish first content · 🤝 Deepen 3 key relationships · 🖥️ Upgrade workspace · 🎨 Start 15-min daily creative practice

  3. Month 3: Accelerate

    📊 Evaluate experiments (double down or kill) · 🏆 Complete learning sprint & ship output · 📋 Retake Future-Proof Score · 🗓️ Plan next 90 days

📝 The 15-Minute Weekly Review

1. Score the Week (2 min)
Rate each dimension 1-5: Career, Money, Health, Relationships, Growth, Purpose.
2. Three Wins (2 min)
What went well? Write three things, no matter how small. Trains your brain to notice progress.
3. One Lesson (2 min)
What did you learn or what would you do differently?
4. Habit Check (3 min)
Did you hit your non-negotiables? Missed twice in a row = red flag.
5. Next Week's #1 (3 min)
The single most important thing. Not most urgent — most important.
6. Energy Check (3 min)
How's your energy? Low energy = something needs to change before burnout hits.

⚠️ Common Failure Modes

Failure Mode Symptom Fix
🔥 Overcommitting Optimize all 6 dimensions at once, burn out by week 3 Pick TWO focus dimensions per quarter. Others get maintenance only.
✨ Shiny Object Syndrome New tool/idea every week, finish nothing Commit for 90 days. "Next quarter" list for new ideas.
🪞 Perfectionism Paralysis Don't ship because "not good enough" Ship at 80%. Done beats perfect. Every. Single. Time.
🏝️ Isolation Executing alone, no accountability Share targets with one person. Monthly check-in.

🤝 Accountability Systems

👥
Accountability Partner
One person, weekly/biweekly check-ins. Share targets, share progress, be honest about misses. Mutual works best.
📢
Public Commitment
Post goals on Twitter, blog, or team Slack. Social pressure motivates. Update monthly.
💸

Bet money on it — StickK or Beeminder. Donate to a cause you dislike if you fail. Sounds extreme, works well.
🏘️
Community
Join or create a group pursuing similar goals — mastermind, Slack, cohort. Seeing others progress is contagious.

📊 Annual Review: The 10 Questions

  1. What am I most proud of?

    Celebrate wins before analyzing gaps.

  2. Biggest lesson or failure?

    The growth is in the mistakes, not the successes.

  3. Stop / Start / Continue?

    What to drop, what to begin, what to keep doing.

  4. Is my career moat still strong?

    Has the AI landscape shifted my positioning?

  5. Who had the most positive impact?

    Name 5 people. Thank them. Invest in those relationships.

✅ Quick Quiz

Why is 90 days the ideal planning horizon?

Long enough to make real progress. Short enough to stay focused. Creates a rhythm: plan → execute → review → repeat. Four cycles per year, each building on the last. And it allows course-correction as the AI landscape shifts.

What are the six dimensions of the Life Strategy Canvas?

Career (Ch 1-2), Money (Ch 3, 6), Health (Ch 7), Relationships (Ch 8), Growth (Ch 4, 5, 9), and Purpose (Ch 10, 11). For each: current state (1-5), 90-day target, and #1 lever.

Why should you focus on only TWO dimensions per quarter?

Trying to optimize all six simultaneously leads to burnout by week 3 (Failure Mode #1: Overcommitting). Two focus dimensions get real effort; the others get maintenance only. Over four quarters, every dimension gets deep attention.

What's the "never miss twice" rule?

One bad week is normal — life happens. Two bad weeks in a row is a pattern that needs attention. Three is a system that needs redesign. Build "grace days" into your accountability — flexibility without abandonment.

What separates people who change their lives from those who don't?

Not talent or resources — it's showing up imperfectly, week after week, for long enough that the compound effect kicks in. 60% execution beats 0% planning. Progress over perfection. Momentum over milestones.

📋 Key Takeaways

  • Strategy without execution is wishful thinking. The 90-day cycle turns ideas into action.
  • Life Strategy Canvas: rate 6 dimensions (Career, Money, Health, Relationships, Growth, Purpose). Pick 2 to focus on per quarter.
  • Month 1 = Foundation (health, finances, career moat, learning sprint). Month 2 = Build (side income, content, relationships). Month 3 = Accelerate (evaluate, double down, plan next cycle).
  • The 15-minute weekly review every Sunday is your steering wheel. Without it, the plan is just a wish list.
  • Four failure modes: overcommitting, shiny objects, perfectionism paralysis, isolation. Know your trap and build the fix in advance.
  • Ship at 80%. Done beats perfect. Every. Single. Time.
  • Accountability: tell one person your goals. Social commitment dramatically increases follow-through.
  • Never miss twice. One bad week is normal. Two in a row needs attention. Three needs system redesign.
  • You are not surviving the AI era — you are designed to thrive in it. Start today.
